How To Play

Tic Tac Toe

1. Tic-tac-toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid.

2. Players take turns marking spaces with their chosen symbol, typically "X" for one player and "O" for the other.

3. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins the game.

4. If all spaces on the grid are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.

5. To play, simply click on an empty space on the grid to place your symbol and try to form a winning line before your opponent.

Stone Paper Scissors

1. Stone, Paper, Scissors is a game where you compete against the computer in a series of rounds.

2. To start, simply click on one of the icons representing stone, paper, or scissors.

3. The computer will then randomly select its choice, and the winner of the round is determined based on the rules: stone crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, paper covers stone.

4. The game consists of multiple rounds, and the player with the most wins at the end is declared the overall winner.

5. Can you outsmart the computer and emerge victorious in this fun and challenging game of strategy and chance?
